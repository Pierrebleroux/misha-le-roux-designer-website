Hello

My name is Misha le Roux and I am a graphic and web designer, who thrives on creative exploration, world-class ideas and beautiful design.

I approach each new project with a different perspective to try to understand the problem and solve it. During the process of finding the solution, I research the topic, learn as much as I can about it, sketch, write ideas down, start with mockups and prototyping ideas through user flows, wireframing and create a specific style guide. This allows me to create a product that has gone through a tough selection process.

Outside of my work, I like to learn through new experiences and opportunities. I think that there will always be something new to learn and understand, especially in a rapid, growing digital environment.
